Salami pizza

Description

Pizza is a well known Italian dish and is eaten all over the world. Everyone's recipe differs from another's and there are many recipes for many different kinds of pizza

This will be my take on the salami pizza, which is one of the most popular variation of this street food.

Ingredients

For this recipe you will need these ingredients:

  • Dough(homemade or store-bought)
  • Tomato sauce of your preference
  • Cheese(also of your preference)
  • Salami sausage
  • Oregano and basil(for garnishing)

Steps

  1. Roll out the dough in your prefered size onto a table
  2. Take the tomato sauce and spread it across the dough with circular motions with a spoon leaving an approximately 1cm wide area on the outside of the dough uncovered
  3. Grate some cheese(and by some I mean enough for covering the sauce area twice)
  4. Scatter the cheese across the sauce-covered area almost fully covering the sauce
  5. Slice up the salami in thin, round slices
  6. Put as much salami on the cheese as you wish
  7. Scatter some more cheese on top of the salami
  8. Scatter a little bit of oregano on the pizza
  9. Heat up the oven to about 185 degrees Celsius and once it's hot, put the pizza in the oven
  10. Bake it until the dough on the side is golden brown and take it out of the oven
  11. Garnish it with some basil
  12. Cut the pizza in 8 slices

The pizza is ready to eat. Bon appetit!
