Morning oatmeal

This is a saturated morning oatmeal to have before a long day at work or school.

Ingredients

For this recipe you will need these ingredients:

  • Milk
  • Oats
  • Cinnamon
  • Fruits

Steps

  1. Boil the milk on medium heat until you can see steam coming out
  2. Add your oats until it forms a little mountain above the milk level in the middle of your pot
  3. Turn down the heat a little bit and lowly whisk it the content
  4. Once you see the oats taking up more space than the milk, add cinnamon to taste
  5. Tun off the heat and put a lid on the pot
  6. Once the oats have swollen up, they are ready
  7. Put the oatmeal in a bowl and add your favorite fruits in top of the oatmeal

The oatmeal is ready. Enjoy your breakfast!
